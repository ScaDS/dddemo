The computational requirements and the trade-off between detection of drift and compute effort is currently investigated in the research community.
Werner et al. have published a website for their ongoing work that considers concept drift detection in a benchmark using Bayesian optimization to identify drift detector configurations that balance between runtime and accuracy of a machine learning model in the data processing pipeline.
